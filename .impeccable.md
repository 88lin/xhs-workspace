## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are knowledge management users and content creators working around Xiaohongshu. They want to collect scattered inspiration, structure it into a reusable library, and turn that material into publishable content without relying on technical tools or local command-line workflows.

### Brand Personality
Refined, deliberate, calm. The product should feel like a polished creative desk rather than a hacker utility. The interface should create confidence and clarity, with just enough warmth to feel crafted instead of sterile.

### Aesthetic Direction
Default to light mode with dark mode support. The visual tone should feel editorial and precise: warm paper-like surfaces, elegant typography, disciplined spacing, and restrained Xiaohongshu-adjacent accents rather than loud platform red everywhere. Avoid generic AI dashboard aesthetics, neon gradients, and over-carded admin layouts.

### Design Principles
1. Build for clarity first: every screen should make the next action obvious.
2. Make the product feel crafted: typography, rhythm, and color should feel intentional and high-end.
3. Keep the shell calm while letting key actions stand out.
4. Treat collection, organization, and creation as one continuous workflow.
5. Preserve lightness: the app should feel approachable to non-technical users even when the underlying system is complex.
